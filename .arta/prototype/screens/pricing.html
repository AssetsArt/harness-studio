// questions, answered
$ what counts as a run?

One agent invocation through Helix, from the call to its final step, no matter how many model or tool hops it takes inside.

$ do I pay Helix for tokens?

No. You bring your own provider keys and pay them directly. Helix bills only for the runtime: routing, tracing and governance.

$ can I self-host?

Yes. Enterprise deploys into your own VPC or on-prem, so runs and traces never leave your network. The runtime ships as a container.
